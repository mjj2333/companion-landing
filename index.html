Privacy & Security

leaves your device.

We don't collect, store, or transmit your data. There are no servers. No accounts. No analytics. Every piece of sensitive information is encrypted on your device with keys only you hold.

Sensitive fields are individually encrypted at rest. Even if your device is accessed, raw data is unreadable.

FAQ

Common
questions.

Is my data really stored only on my device?
Yes. Companion has zero servers. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics. All your data lives in your browser's local storage, encrypted with keys that never leave your device.
What happens if I lose my phone?
Since data is stored locally, losing your device means losing your data — unless you've exported a backup. We recommend regularly using the backup/export feature. Even if someone finds your phone, the PIN lock and encryption protect your information.
Is it really free? What's the catch?
Companion is completely free to use with all core features. There's no catch — we believe safety and privacy tools should be accessible to everyone. A future Pro tier may add advanced features, but the core app will always remain free.
What is stealth mode?
Stealth mode disguises the entire app interface as a basic calculator. Anyone looking at your screen will see a fully functional calculator — they won't know there's a CRM underneath. Enter your PIN in the calculator to unlock the real app.
What is a duress PIN?
A duress PIN is a special code that, when entered instead of your real PIN, silently and irreversibly wipes all app data. It's designed for situations where you're forced to unlock your device. The app will appear empty as if freshly installed.
Does it work on iPhone and Android?
Yes. Companion is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on any modern browser — iPhone, Android, tablet, laptop, or desktop. Just open the link and tap "Add to Home Screen" to install it like a native app.
